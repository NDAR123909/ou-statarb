# Weekly research log

`deploy/report_weekly.py` writes one entry here every Monday and never edits an older one. Each entry lists the week's objective metrics from the committed track record: returns, trade and fill counts, stop-outs, drawdown, workflow health, and whether anyone touched the record by hand. It carries no market commentary by design. The raw data under `track_record/` and its git history stay the source of truth; `VERIFY.md` explains how to audit them.
